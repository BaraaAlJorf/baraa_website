About Me

I am a Computer Science PhD researcher at New York University and a Visiting Researcher at the University of Oxford's AI4DH lab. My work lies at the intersection of multimodal machine learning and healthcare, with a strong focus on clinical foundation models. I am passionate about building safe, agentic AI systems that synthesize complex data like clinical notes, medical imaging, and electronic health records to assist in critical tasks like diagnosis and risk prediction. Recently, I also spent a semester as a Visiting PhD student at Columbia University to deepen my foundation in causal inference and foundational LLM design.
